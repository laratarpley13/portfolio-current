About Me

Hi, I am a web developer currently based in Austin, TX.

After completing my undergratuate degree in Economics, I decided that I did not want to pursue it as a career field and was determined to find a field that I was passionate about and get a lot of joy from. So, while I was exploring my options I started playing around with code again in my free time and exploring resources online about web development and I found myself wanting to keep challenging myself to make better web applications and now, after a lot of learning about code, I am pursuing a career as a web developer.

I think web development is a wonderful mix of both creativity and logic and a field in which you will always be learning something new, and those are major things that drew me to the field, as I am always ready for my next coding challege and watching my work come to life with the help and support of a team of fellow web developers.

Outside of coding, you will most likely find me in the kitchen developing and creating new recipes and dishes to share with friends and family or enjoying the outdoors whether that is a nice hike or reading a good book at a park.

Projects

CookIt

Live App Client Repo API Repo

I love to cook and create new recipes and wanted a platform that would make it easy for anyone to share their recipe creations and recieve constructive feedback on the recipes in the form of pictures, as I feel like food can be a very visual experience.

This app allows users to create their own account and explore recipes, with the option to filter by category or dietary restrictions, and try other people's recipes and leave them feedback as well.

Technology Front-end: React, ReactRouter, HTML, CSs

Techonology API: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
